The Vision

Tony Bautista

With over a decade of dedicated service in the St. Louis area, Tony has transformed hundreds of residential spaces into functional works of art.

Our philosophy is simple: prioritize structural longevity and timeless design. We don't just plant flowers; we engineer environments that evolve with your property.

Founded in 2022, Tony's Landscape has been dedicated to creating and maintaining beautiful landscapes with a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Our team of experienced horticulturists and designers works closely with you to turn your outdoor dreams into reality. We believe in sustainable practices, using eco-friendly materials and methods whenever possible.
